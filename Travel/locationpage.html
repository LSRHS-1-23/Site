The North Pole is a great location to spend your vacation. Some people worry about the cold weather but it only gets to -29 degrees fahrenheit in the winter and it even gets up to 32 degrees fahrenheit in the summer! The cold has not seemed to bother any of the visitors because Santa and his elves take extra precautions to make sure the people staying there have a great vacation.
